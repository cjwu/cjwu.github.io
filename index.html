Biography

Hi, there. Chi-Jen Wu (CJ) currently is an assistant professor of the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering at Chang Gung University, Taiwan since February 2021. He received his Electrical Engineering Ph.D. from National Taiwan University in July 2012. He worked as a Distinguished Postdoctoral Scholar of the Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica from 2012 to 2013. His research interests include Content Distribution, Mobile Cloud Computing, and Artificial Intelligence with a specific focus on Computational Advertising, Marketing Automation, and Financial Computing. He is a member of IEEE and ACM.

CJ cofounded Fiiser Inc (2013), worked on App search engine, App virtualization and streaming technology, and a large-scale cloud management system for Andriod VM. He also cofounded Koodata Inc (2019), focusing on social live commerce and advertising intelligence. CJ served on her board until 2020. In the 7 years journey of startup venture, he worked with a super talented team, maked things and had fun with them. He enjoys himself making things and solving real-world problems.

Mission statement

We study foundational scientific principles that enable network systems to make people reaching their interested information handily and efficiently. The research topics in Knowledge & Data Engineering and Networking System include:

1) discover interested information, e.g., business intelligence for E-Commerce

2) publish information from the sources, e.g., social live, sending e-mail

3) obtain information at the destinations, e.g., watching Netflix, google search
